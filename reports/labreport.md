# Lab Report: Demystifying Quantum Coherence Protection

## Core claim

The same mechanical interaction that enables quantum information transfer can also help preserve that information.

## Context

Silicon-vacancy centers in diamond are promising quantum systems because their electron spins couple strongly to strain.

## Observation

Continuous coherent mechanical driving creates noise-resistant dressed spin states compatible with phononic cavities.

## Result

The paper reports ultrafast mechanically driven spin control with Rabi frequencies approaching 800 MHz.

## Climate Reality

Climate reality describes what engineering prescribes: identify constraints, then build within them.
